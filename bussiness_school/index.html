Welcome to GMR School of Business!

GMR School of Business was established in 2013 by GMR Group with the partnership of Schulich School of Business (York University), Toronto, Canada - it offers two years full time MBA course, accredited by AICTE, India. The campus is located next to the Hyderabad International Airport, Shamshabad, Hyderabad.

About GMR

GMR Group is one of the fastest growing infrastructure enterprises in the country with interests in Airports, Energy, Highways and Urban Infrastructure sectors. Employing the Public Private Partnership model, the Group has successfully implemented several iconic infrastructure projects in India. The Group also has a global presence with infrastructure operating assets and projects in several countries including Turkey, South Africa, Indonesia, Singapore.

About Schulich School of Business

RANKED #1 IN CANADA by The Economist, Financial Times, Forbes, and QS, Schulich is Canada’s preeminent business school. Global, innovative, and diverse, the Schulich School of Business is rated among the world’s leading business schools. A Schulich MBA offers a flexible, accessible, intellectually challenging and transformative learning experience, preparing you to become an effective and purpose-driven leader who aspires to make a positive contribution to business and society. Customize your own unique career path by choosing from one of 18 MBA specializations or let us prepare you to kickstart your own business venture. Acquire vital real-world experience working with clients on our capstone strategic consulting course and gain the latest in management learning and practice developed at one of our many cutting-edge labs, research hubs and Centres of Excellence.
